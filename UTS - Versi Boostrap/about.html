LYVESTA 2025

LYVESTA is more than just a music festival. It’s a celebration of sound, light, and culture. Born from the fusion of K-Pop’s vibrant energy and Western pop’s soulful rhythm, LYVESTA unites two worlds into one unforgettable stage. Here, every beat connects people from different places, languages, and stories. Turning music into a universal language of joy.
